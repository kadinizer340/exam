Upload rules

Prepare the Excel files correctly.

  • Use `.xls` or `.xlsx` only.
  • Every sheet tab should represent a class or section such as `csa` or `ec`.
  • Each sheet must contain `rollnum` and `name` columns exactly.
  • Keep `rollnum` numeric so lookup and seat generation continue to work.
  • After upload, the system stores each sheet name as the student group identifier.
